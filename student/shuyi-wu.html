Project Title:
Uniform.

shuyiwu.cargo.site
@shu1_is_shuyi
Description:
This project explores the quiet complexity hidden beneath visual sameness. While individuality is often linked to standing out, many people find comfort in blending in. Looking alike on the outside doesnt mean we are the same within. This project reflects on how internal difference, emotion, and identity can exist quietly without loud expression or visual contrast. It does not offer answers, but invites reflection on how we interpret sameness, and what we might overlook when we only focus on what we see.
